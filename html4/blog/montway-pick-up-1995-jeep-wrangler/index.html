Montway picking up 1995 Jeep Wrangler

Mike Mihaylov/February 5, 2013August 31, 2016 /Leave a comment

Montway arranged the transport for this 1995 Jeep Wrangler. The vehicle was shipped from Utica, MI to Coloma, MI. It is an operable car and we wondered why he needs to transport it, but hey it is customer’s choice after all. Customer booked his order with Montway on Dec 4th and the first available pick up date was Dec 11th. The Jeep was picked up on Dec 17thand delivered the next day. Customer requested delivery before noon and we did accommodate his request. He was happy to film the pick up of his vehicle – the whole process that took about ten minutes. In most cases customer film only the actual loading or unloading of the truck, so, we are quite happy with this video. Enjoy!
