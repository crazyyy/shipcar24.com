Choose When You Transport Your Car

Safe, secure, and expedited auto transport — move when you choose!

Auto transport requires the coordination of several factors, making timeliness and flexibility necessary components. With Expedited Transport, you can feel at ease and have your car picked up on the exact date of your move.

Making a last minute decision? With Expedited Car Shipping, you can get your car transported as soon as 24-48 hours after ordering.

The premium paid for this service is a small price to pay for the added convenience and satisfaction that comes along with express shipping. The transporter will be rearranging his or her route to accommodate your time-sensitive pickup.

Shipping Cost

Price depends on several factors including: seasonal supply and demand, flexibility of dates, car type, car condition, location, distance, and transport type (open or enclosed car transport).

Priority Care

By choosing expedited shipping, you are accelerating the car transportation process. Car carriers will consider the delivery of your vehicle as a top priority and make necessary accommodations.

Limitations and Extras

Remote or rural pickup often requires the extra services of a local towing company. This method is used as a last resort, because it may lead to prolonged delivery times. Please ask one of our agents if you think this limitation may apply to you.

*Expedited Transit Times may be estimated, but not guaranteed.
