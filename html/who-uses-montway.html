Who Uses Auto Transport?

Most Common Shippers

You Can Rely on Montway

We offer a number of specialized services designed to accommodate
the unique needs of every customer and car.

Montway is an industry authority. Our team has moved hundreds of thousands of cars across the country for people of all walks of life. Whether you buy cars online as a hobby, are relocating because of a new deployment, job, or home, or simply spending your winter in warmer climates – we are the auto transport experts to serve your needs.

Moving & Relocating

Whether moving to a new home or gearing up for a long vacation, your car goes with you.

  • Check off one less important item to worry about getting to your new home
  • Save time and money
  • Don't waste days behind the wheel

Montway is a trusted partner for some of the largest Moving & Relocation companies in the country.

Purchasing Online

Buying a car online is easy these days, from a dealership, eBay, or online retailer, but what if the car you're seeking is several states away?

  • No distance restriction on finding the perfect car
  • Save money by finding the right price or buying in a state with no sales tax
  • Ship instead of driving to avoid plane fare, mileage, and wear and tear

Montway is an eBay Premier Carrier and the exclusive transport partner for Swap-A-Lease and Auto Trader Classics.

Collectors & Enthusiasts

Some cars are more about drive than driving — passion for powerful engines and innovative design, rapture for rich history and classic engineering.

  • Find your dream car, anywhere in the country
  • Shipping includes insurance--don't add a long-distance drive to your precious find
  • Know your car is in great hands with truckers who regularly move special-care vehicles

Auto transport may not be a form of art, but we take pride in our ability to ship and preserve yours.

Snowbirds

People who fly to warmer climates for the winter, known as "snowbirds," usually bring their cars along.

  • Shipping is the safest and most economical way to take your own car
  • Cross-country travel can be dangerous, especially during these high-traffic and inclement weather seasons
  • Driving means additional costs for food, gas, and hotels

It's not worth the risk. Let Montway deliver your car south and bring it home for you, too.

Military

When you receive your PCS order, the last thing you need to worry about is getting your car to your new home.

  • Simplest and most secure auto relocation service available
  • Fit your timeframe with guaranteed pickup dates and expedited shipping
  • Honoring your service with ours!

We're always looking to help out with any discounts that may be available for Active Duty, Retirees, Veterans, Reserve/National Guard, and Military Dependents. It's the least we can do for the brave men and women who protect our country.

Students

When it's time to live and learn at a college or university, students often head out of state.

  • Ensure your student's car will get between home and campus, safe and secure
  • Skip the risk of your student spending a long time on the road
  • Avoid the inconvenience of navigating and parking while getting settled in

Give yourself one less thing to worry about when your student moves away. Ship with Montway and seize the day!
